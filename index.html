      HOW DO I SELECT MY SEAT IN ADVANCE?

      For packages booked to Mexico & the Caribbean, as well as Las Vegas, Hawaii, California and Orlando, you can select your seat at time of booking online. If your upcoming vacation destination is in Mexico, the Caribbean, Las Vegas, Hawaii, California or Orlando, visit our Manage Your Booking page to add your seat selection.

      For all other destinations, call us at 1-866-529-2079 after completing your booking to reserve your seat in advance. Please have your confirmation number handy.

      IS THERE AN ADDITIONAL COST FOR ADVANCE SEAT SELECTION?

      The price for advance seat selection varies according to seat type and destination as follows:

      Economy Class
      The price for advance seat selection varies based on a number of factors, such as the selected seat type, destination, and flight segment.

      Example: For a consumer flying in Economy Class from Montreal to Jamaica with a connection in Toronto, there will be a cost for advance seat selection per flight segment (i.e., the Montreal to Toronto segment, the Toronto to Jamaica segment, and also for the return segments from Jamaica to Toronto and Toronto to Montreal). A trip like this would have four segments, each of which would incur a separate cost. Consumers are able to choose which segments they would like to add advance seat selection to.

      Business Class
      Complimentary advance seat selection for all destinations.

      HOW DO I JOIN AEROPLAN?

      Becoming an Aeroplan Member is easy and free! Simply register at aircanada.com/joinaeroplan to receive your Aeroplan membership number and to start earning points for purchases you make with Air Canada Vacations and many other partners.

      HOW CAN I REDEEM MY AEROPLAN POINTS TO BOOK WITH AIR CANADA VACATIONS?

      You can book your vacation by redeeming points for vacation packages, Flight & Hotel bundles, hotels, tours and cruises*.

      • Sign in with your Aeroplan account
      • Once signed in, search for your destination.
      • If booking vacations in Mexico, the Caribbean, Hawaii, Las Vegas, Los Angeles or Orlando, select the number of points you would like to redeem from the drop-down menu at the left of your search results.
      • If booking vacations in Canada, Europe, or cities in the United States not listed above, select the number of points you would like to redeem by adjusting the slider above your search results or at the bottom of the checkout page.
      • Start shopping for your next vacation!

      *To redeem your Aeroplan points to book a cruise, please contact our Reservations Department at 1 866 529-2079. Please note that your points will be deducted once you complete your booking.
